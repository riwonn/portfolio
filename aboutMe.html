I'm a Product Designer, Riwon Kwon.

Some consider the essence of humanity to be 'compassion.' To coexist with others, it's said that we must bring benefit to those around us. Design shares a common thread in this aspect, as it's a process of striving to provide benefits to others. Through design, I aim to become a product designer who allows oneself, collaborators, and users to mutually gain benefits from one another.

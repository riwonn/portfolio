OUNCE OUNCE is a food tracking application designed for individuals who keep records of their cats' diets. Previously, people either manually recorded information or typed it out in various note-taking apps, which often proved inconvenient. To address this issue, we conducted research on common practices and preferences among pet owners, enabling us to create a user-friendly system for recording and easily accessing essential information. This application allows users to record specific details, which are used to classify and filter the food's quality. To promote this, we opened a website and Instagram.
Pain point Through data analysis, surveys, and interviews, we gained insights into people's concerns, as well as their criteria for selecting and recording pet food choices. According to the research, the most crucial factor when choosing pet food is their 'pet's preference.' This is particularly challenging to fulfill, hence emphasizing the importance of keeping records. The difficulties encountered in recording pet food choices include the following:
Too many records Despite having kept track of products that are enjoyed by trying various items, it becomes difficult to remember as the number of these records increases.
Not intuitive In the mobile phone notepads used by 88% of cat owners, recording foreign-language products in abbreviated form makes it difficult to grasp intuitively.
Solution
Record through a user-friendly mobile app
Systematically record and retrieve using features such as photos, sorting, filters, and hashtags
Register and manage multiple cats for multi-cat households
Design & Lunching Based on planning and research, we proceeded with app design. Reflecting the preferences of the target audience, women aged 20 to 30, we chose a cute cat character as the main design. The concept revolves around the 'journey to discover tastes,' and as records accumulate, the cat on the main screen embarks on new adventures. Users can rate their pets' preferences, leave tags and notes related to pet food, and keep track of their records. The recorded information can be filtered based on preference and submission order.
The application has been released for both iOS and Android platforms.
App Execution Screen - 3 Modes
Promotion We executed three strategies for app promotion with the aim of reaching the target demographic of women aged 20 to 30, in order to increase Monthly Active Users (MAU). This approach was intended to enhance app visibility and engagement within the specified target audience.
New Users Website - We designed a personality test website related to the app, allowing users to naturally encounter the application.
Existing Users Instagram - Through Instagram, we promote app features and provide update notifications, consistently delivering information.
Core Users Reviews - We expand our core user base by sharing usage experiences and methods in cat-related blogs and communities, along with user reviews.
Information Architecture This is an Information Architecture showing the services coming after the description and storytelling of the game. Simple and interesting content is configured according to preferred persona.
Since the main target of "KoLing" is foreigners all over the world, not for Koreans, therefore we chose the IOS operating system that takes a higher market share. It is designed according to the IOS grid.
